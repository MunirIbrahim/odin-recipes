Pomegrante Rice Salad

Flecked with your choice of rice white, brown or basmati easy Holiday side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked grain rice(any kind optional)
  • 2teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 bunch scallions(white and green parts choped)
  • Cloves garlic(minced)
  • 1/3 cup toasted(chopped pistachios)
  • Herbs mint leaves and finely chpped parsley fleck

Method

  1. Start by cooking the rice
  2. let the rice cool and whisk together the dressing
  3. lightly saute the scallions and garlic and some parsley and stir the cooked rice in to the pan
  4. Transfer the rice salad to a serving dish, drizzle it with more dressing and top it with more parsley, mint and fresh pmegrante
  5. serve at room temperature or slightly warm enjoy!
